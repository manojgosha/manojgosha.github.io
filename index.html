I am Swati Jindal, a fourth year Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz, working with Prof. Roberto Manduchi. My research interests mainly lies in computer vision and deep learning, with specific interest in image- or video-based gaze tracking.

Previously, I was a Researcher at TCS Innovation Labs, India working with Dr. Gautam Shroff and Dr. Lovekesh Vig. I completed my Master's degree from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Hyderabad working with Prof. K Sri Rama Murty.
